Who We Are

Water damage often returns when the repair only covers the visible surface. Our approach is to locate the real water path, repair cracks and joints, then protect the surface with the correct waterproofing system.

We handle residential and commercial projects including roofs, terraces, balconies, concrete slabs, damp walls and wet areas.

✓ Source-based leak diagnosis
✓ Professional application and clean finishing
✓ Waterproofing for interior and exterior surfaces
✓ Solutions for homes, buildings and commercial spaces
